February 2023

The beginning

LLaMA 1 Released

Meta releases 4 model sizes: 7B, 13B, 33B, 65B parameters. Outperforms GPT-3 despite being smaller.

Open weights Research only

March 2023 — The Leak

A user posts LLaMA weights on 4chan. Within hours, everyone has it. Suddenly anyone can run a GPT-class model locally. Free. Forever.

Went viral Game changer

July 2023

Bigger and commercial

LLaMA 2

Commercial license available (with terms). Larger context (4k → 8k). Better training with RLHF. Chat fine-tune included.

Open weights Commercial OK

LLaMA 2 Powers the Ecosystem

Alpaca, Vicuna, LlamaCpp, Ollama, LM Studio — everyone builds on it. Local AI becomes mainstream.

Ecosystem boom
