HexClamp: What It Is & How It Works

Explanation

What is HexClamp?

An autonomous loop that keeps running, learning, and improving. Forever.

How It Works

OBSERVE Look around. What's happening now?

CONDENSE Filter noise. Keep what's relevant.

PLAN Decide what to do next.

EXECUTE Run the plan.

VERIFY Did it work? Find problems?

PERSIST Save results for next loop.

The Loops

After PERSIST → back to OBSERVE (main loop).

If VERIFY fails → back to PLAN (retry loop).

Flow

OBSERVE
↓
CONDENSE
↓
PLAN
↓
EXECUTE
↓
